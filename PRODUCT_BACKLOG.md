# Private Business Finance OS - Prioritized Backlog

## Now (Build Immediately)
1. Month Close Hub
- Close readiness score
- Uncategorized, needs-review, missing-split counters
- Scope-aware (selected month and date range)

2. Partner Split Overview
- Net allocation to partner vs business
- Partner split ratio on expense base
- Expense base and credits applied

3. Vendor Concentration
- Top 5 vendors by spend
- Concentration percentage of total expense base
- Visual bars for quick scan

## Next (High ROI)
1. Reconciliation workflow
- Opening/closing balance checks
- Unmatched and duplicate queue
- Month lock after close

2. Budget vs Actual
- Monthly category budget
- Variance reporting by category and by partner split
- Alert when category overspend crosses threshold

3. Rule quality analytics
- Auto-tag precision tracking
- Manual override rate by category/vendor
- Suggested rule creation from repeated edits

## Later (Scale and Control)
1. Advanced forecasting
- 13-week cash flow forecast
- Scenario planning (base, conservative, aggressive)

2. Audit and approvals
- Approval gates for month close
- Full audit timeline for category/split changes

3. Accountant package
- One-click export bundle per month
- Reconciliation summary and adjustment log

## North Star Metrics
1. Auto-categorization precision > 90%
2. Month-close completion time < 2 hours
3. Unreconciled transaction rate < 1%
4. Manual override rate trending down month over month
